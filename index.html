About Us

BUS is a 12-member Thai boy group under SONRAY MUSIC, formed from the finalists of the survival show 789 SURVIVAL. They officially debuted on December 6, 2023, with their hit single Because of You I Shine, which inspired the group’s name, “BUS.”

In 2024, BUS held a mini fan meeting tour across Thai provinces like Chiang Mai, Korat, Khon Kaen, Hat Yai, and Si Racha, connecting with fans from September 28 to October 27. They will celebrate their first anniversary concert on December 6, 2024, followed by their first full-scale concert on March 15, 2025.

Fans can also enjoy seeing BUS off-stage through their YouTube variety show Bussing Thailand, where the members showcase their bonds, laughter, and heartfelt moments.
